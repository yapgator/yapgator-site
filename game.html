CURRENT ANDROID PROTOTYPE

The version available today is an early gameplay prototype. It was created to establish the Android release system, introduce the first Gator Egg, Swamp Shop, Locker, and gameplay systems, and place a playable Yapgator experience directly into the community’s hands.

This is not the completed game. It does not represent the final artwork, camera system, world design, combat, animation quality, or complete Story Mode.

We released the prototype so supporters could see Yapgator become playable while development continues on the larger experience envisioned for YAPGATOR: SWAMP SIEGE.

The next major version is planned as an original first-person 3D story-driven mobile action-adventure.
