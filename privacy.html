PRIVACY AND SAFETY

YAPGATOR PRIVACY POLICY

Effective July 18, 2026

What the website and app do

The Yapgator website and native Android app display official project status, announcements, safety information and official external links. Status and announcement information is downloaded from official Yapgator infrastructure.

Wallet and financial safety

The website and app do not request seed phrases or private keys, do not connect to wallets, do not custody funds and do not execute trades. Official destinations open externally. Yapgator administrators will never request passwords or Telegram login codes.

Local data

Notification preferences may be stored locally. Cached status and announcements may be stored locally for offline access and reliability. The app does not include an advertising SDK. No personal analytics is enabled by default, and Yapgator does not sell personal data.

External services

Telegram and external websites have their own privacy practices. Review their policies before using them.

Contact and verification

Verify official information through yapgator.com and @YapgatorOfficial.
